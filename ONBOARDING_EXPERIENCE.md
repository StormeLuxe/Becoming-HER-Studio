# ONBOARDING EXPERIENCE — Becoming Profile Journey

## Philosophy

This should feel like a **TRANSFORMATION JOURNEY** not a form. Every design decision must reinforce: *"She didn't arrive... she became."*

---

## IMPLEMENTATION REQUIREMENTS:

### 1. PROGRESS BAR at the top of every step:
- Show "Step X of 7" 
- Visual progress bar that fills as they advance
- Completed steps show a checkmark
- Labels: Identity ✓ → Brand → Audience → Content → Visual → Voice → Goals

---

### 2. STEP 1 — WHO ARE YOU BECOMING?
- **Headline:** "Who Are You Becoming?"
- **Subtext:** "Choose what resonates. You can always evolve."
- **Clickable cards (allow multiple selection):**
  * Content Creator — Build content across platforms
  * Influencer — Build a personal brand and audience
  * Business Owner — Grow a business through content
  * Coach / Consultant — Attract clients, build authority
  * AI Creator — Create digital personalities and AI brands
  * Author / Storyteller — Create books, scripts, stories
  * Digital Product Seller — Sell courses, templates, tools
- Each card has icon, title, description
- Selected cards glow pink with checkmark

---

### 3. STEP 2 — YOUR BRAND
- **Section intro card:**
  "What are you building? This helps personalize every AI generation."
- **Fields with example placeholders:**
  * Brand Name (example: "Storme Luxe")
  * Tagline (example: "She didn't arrive... she became.")
  * Your Niche (example: "AI creator education for women")
  * Target Audience (example: "Women 25-45 building digital brands")
  * Brand Mission (example: "Helping women become the next version of themselves")

---

### 4. STEP 3 — VISUAL IDENTITY
- **Section intro:** "How should your brand look?"
- **Visual style cards with emoji + name + description:**
  * ✨ Soft Luxury — Elegant, feminine, warm

  * 🔥 Bold & Confident — Strong, powerful, direct
  * 🎬 Cinematic — Dramatic, storytelling, visual
  * 🌸 Feminine — Soft, romantic, approachable
  * 🖤 Minimal — Clean, modern, understated
- Single selection
- **Color palette field** with placeholder:
  "e.g. Black, crimson, gold, blush pink"

---

### 5. STEP 4 — CONTENT IDENTITY
- **"Where do you create?"**
- **Platform toggles (multi-select):**
  Instagram / TikTok / YouTube / Facebook / Pinterest / LinkedIn / Fanvue / Podcast
- **"What are your content goals?" (multi-select chips):**
  Growth / Sales / Authority / Community / Entertainment / Education / Inspiration

### 6. STEP 5 — YOUR VOICE
- **"How should your content feel?"**
- **Selectable buttons (multi-select):**
  Educational / Luxury / Inspirational / Confident / Storytelling / Motivational / Empowering / Intimate / Bold / Soft

  placeholder: "e.g. She didn't arrive... she became."

  * Personal Brand

---

## DESIGN REQUIREMENTS:

- Dark background #05010A
- Cards use glassmorphism
- Selected state: pink glow + border
- Progress bar gradient: pink → purple → gold
- Each step fades in smoothly
- Back/Next buttons at bottom
- "Skip for now" option on optional fields
- Save to Supabase on every step completion
- Mobile responsive

---

## NAVIGATION:

- Back button on every step except step 1
- Next button only activates when required selection is made
- Can skip optional text fields
- Progress saves automatically

---

## BRAND PHILOSOPHY

Every pixel reinforces: *"She didn't arrive... she became."*

This is not account setup. This is identity activation.
